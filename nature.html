Some of the Best Beaches:

Whites Beach

Whites Beach is a stunning coastal destination in the Marlborough Region. It offers pristine sands and crystal-clear waters, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and water activities.

Milford Beach

Milford Beach is a beautiful coastal destination in Auckland. It offers scenic views and a peaceful atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for relaxation and outdoor activities.

Devonport Beach

Devonport Beach is a charming coastal destination in Auckland. It offers stunning views of the city skyline and a tranquil atmosphere, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and outdoor activities.

Or maybe some hiking and walking?

Auckland Domain

As Auckland’s oldest park, Auckland Domain is a spacious and historic green area right in the heart of the city. Built around an ancient volcanic crater, it features large open fields, walking paths, and beautifully maintained gardens like the Wintergardens. It’s a popular spot for picnics, casual sports, or just relaxing with friends. The park is also home to the Auckland War Memorial Museum, adding a cultural element to your visit. Whether you’re looking for a quiet break or something active, the Domain has something for everyone.

Tahuna Torea Reserve

Tahuna Torea Nature Reserve is a peaceful coastal escape tucked away in East Auckland. Known for its walking tracks and wide open views of the Tāmaki Estuary, it’s a great place for a calm, low-cost day out. The reserve is especially popular for birdwatching, with a variety of native and migratory species often spotted along the shoreline. Its quiet atmosphere makes it ideal for unwinding, going for a walk, or just enjoying nature without the crowds found in more popular locations.

Kitekite Falls

Located near Piha on Auckland’s west coast, Kitekite Falls is a breathtaking multi-tiered waterfall surrounded by lush native bush. The walk to the falls is relatively easy and takes you through scenic forest tracks with plenty of photo-worthy spots along the way. At the base, you’ll find a natural pool where visitors often cool off, especially in summer. It’s a perfect mix of light adventure and relaxation, making it a must-visit for anyone looking to escape the city without spending money.
