Hungry on a budget?

Us too... that’s why we’ve researched for you the best places to eat around that don’t break your bank!

Feeling like a burger?

Burger Burger

Burger Burger is a good pick if you want a more premium burger. Their Beef & Cheese Burger is about $24.38, and the Old Smokey is about $28.75 on current Auckland menu listings. The food is fresh, filling, and well made. It costs more than basic fast food, but the portions and quality make it feel worth it for a treat meal.

Shake Out

Shake Out is a solid choice if you want a simple burger meal in the city. It is known for burgers, fries, and shakes, which makes it a good place to eat with friends. The style is casual and easy, so it works well for lunch or dinner. It feels more relaxed than a fancy restaurant, but still has a fun atmosphere.

Wise Boys

Wise Boys is a great option if you want a plant-based burger that still feels like comfort food. Their Classic Cheese Burger is about $18.90, and fries are about $8.50 on current Auckland menu listings. The burgers are full of flavour and still feel indulgent. It is a good pick for vegetarians, vegans, or anyone who wants something different without giving up the burger experience

Craving Asian?

Eden Noodles

Eden Noodles is a favourite for cheap and filling meals. Popular dishes include Dandan Noodles and Noodles with Spicy Beef in Soup, both listed at about $15.60 on current Auckland menus. The portions are strong for the price, and the flavours are bold. It is a great place to go if you want something warm, spicy, and affordable.

Ramen Takara

Ramen Takara is a good choice if you want a warm and filling bowl of ramen. It is known for rich broth, noodles, and a classic Japanese comfort-food feel. That makes it a strong option on cold days or for an easy dinner. I could not verify a current official price from the sources I found, so I would avoid listing a number for this one unless you check the menu yourself first.

Pocha

Pocha is a lively Korean restaurant with lots of variety. Its menu includes Korean fried chicken, noodles, and snacks, so it works well for sharing with friends. The food is fun, bold, and full of flavour. It is a good choice if you want something more social than a standard takeaway meal.
